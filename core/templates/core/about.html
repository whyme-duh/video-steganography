About The Project


Camouflage - Video/ Image Steganography allows users to encrypt and decrypt a hidden message embedded in a image/video. Users can securely connect with one another and shield the information they send from prying eyes by using this technology. In reality, the conversation may be a secret text messaging between two persons, even if it may seem like a typical video/image sharing conversation.

Users have options to choose which steganography is required for them. The two options are: image steganography and video steganography. Users can try both and use any one of them that is needed as per their requirements. Camouflage also gives the user the feature to download encoded video and images and also allows to export the decoded message in the text file.
